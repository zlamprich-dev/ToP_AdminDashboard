About Me

Hi! I’m Kristi MacFarlane, a tattoo artist based in Denver, Colorado. Art has always been a huge part of my life, but it wasn’t until I discovered tattooing that I truly found my creative voice. There’s something incredibly powerful about transforming ideas into designs that people carry with them forever. Each tattoo I create is a piece of art with its own story, and I love collaborating with my clients to bring their visions to life.
Living in Denver, I’m constantly inspired by the energy of the city and the beauty of the surrounding landscapes. My work is a reflection of both my personal journey and the diverse experiences of those I have the privilege to tattoo.

I see tattooing as more than just a job—it’s a way to connect with people on a deeper level, helping them express their identities and commemorate the moments that matter most. Whether you’re looking for something bold, intricate, or uniquely personal, I’m here to help you turn your ideas into a tattoo that you’ll be proud to wear. Let’s create something meaningful together!
